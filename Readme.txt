This web application represents the CRUD operation for employees and makes operation on salary for the department.
you can get all employees in the database.
you can get all the departments that you have.
you can get all employees depending on the department. 
you can get employee details by his ID.
you can delete an employee from the database.
or you can update his data.
you can increase the salaries of employees based on their department
and choose if this increase will be fixed or a percentage.
